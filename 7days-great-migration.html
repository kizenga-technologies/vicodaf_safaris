7 Day Serengeti Great Migration Safari

7 Day Serengeti Great Migration Safari

Witness Nature’s Greatest Show – Unforgettable. Untamed. Once in a Lifetime. Join us as we follow the thundering hooves of wildebeest and zebras across the plains of Serengeti toward the perilous Mara River crossings during the Great Migration.

📅 Itinerary – Day by Day

Day 1: Arrival in Arusha – Your Safari Journey Begins!

Arrive at Kilimanjaro International Airport. Our team will warmly welcome you and transfer you to your hotel in Arusha. Rest and prepare for your incredible journey ahead.

Day 2: Tarangire National Park – Giants of the Wilderness

Discover Tarangire’s legendary elephant herds and massive baobabs. Keep your camera ready for lions, giraffes, and more in this incredible park.

Day 3: Lake Manyara National Park – Tree-Climbing Lions Await

Explore Lake Manyara’s rich ecosystem, known for flamingos, hippos, and the famous tree-climbing lions. A hidden gem in the Northern Circuit.

Day 4: Serengeti Central – Endless Plains, Endless Wonders

Enter the iconic Serengeti National Park. Witness big cats on the hunt and begin tracking the migration herds across the vast savannah.

Day 5: Serengeti Mara – Follow the Migration's Thunder

Travel north to the Mara River for dramatic river crossings. This is where nature’s raw drama unfolds — with crocodiles waiting and predators lurking.

Day 6: Serengeti to Ngorongoro Crater – The Lost World Awaits

Depart Serengeti and head to Ngorongoro Highlands. Overnight near the crater rim, surrounded by misty forest and breathtaking views.

Day 7: Ngorongoro Crater Exploration – Safari’s Grand Finale

Descend into the crater for your final game drive. Spot rare black rhinos, elephants, and flamingos all in one place. A stunning grand finale to your safari.
