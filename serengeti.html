Serengeti National Park

Discover the Heart of Africa's Wildlife

The Serengeti National Park is the most iconic safari destination in Africa. Spanning over 14,750 square kilometers, it is home to the world-famous Great Migration, where millions of wildebeest, zebras, and gazelles move in a continuous cycle of life and survival. The Serengeti offers endless savannahs, dramatic landscapes, and an unmatched wildlife experience.

At Vicodaf Safaris, we offer curated tours designed to immerse you in the raw beauty of this ancient ecosystem. Whether you're on your first safari or a seasoned explorer, our expert guides and luxury accommodations ensure a journey of a lifetime.

Top Activities in the Serengeti

  • Witness the Great Migration (seasonal)
  • Big Five game drives (lion, elephant, leopard, rhino, buffalo)
  • Sunrise hot air balloon safari over the savannah
  • Visit Maasai villages for a cultural experience
  • Enjoy stunning sunsets and sundowners in the wild

Best Time to Visit

The Serengeti offers great wildlife viewing all year round. However, the best time to witness the migration is from June to October, with calving season occurring from January to March in the southern plains.
