Rongai Route-Kilimanjaro Trekking

Rongai Route – Kilimanjaro Climb

The Rongai Route is the only trail that approaches Kilimanjaro from the north, close to the Kenyan border. This remote and less-traveled route offers a more unspoiled wilderness experience. It is considered one of the easier routes, with a steady ascent and fewer crowds.

5 Days Rongai Route Itinerary

Click to Expand

Day 1: Nalemuru Gate to Simba Camp (2,200m to 2,600m). A 3–4 hour hike through forest to camp just beyond the trees.

Day 2: Simba Camp to Mawenzi Tarn via Kikelewa Camp. Rest at Kikelewa tarn for lunch and reach Mawenzi Tarn by evening.

Day 3: Mawenzi Tarn to Kibo Hut (4700m). A 5–6 hour trek across the Saddle.

Day 4: Midnight summit to Uhuru Peak (5895m) via Gilman’s Point, then descend to Horombo Hut for overnight stay.

Day 5: Descend to Marangu Gate via Mandara Hut, return to hotel.

Price Per Person: $1,350 USD

6 Days Rongai Route Itinerary

Click to Expand

Day 1: Hotel to Simba Camp (2650m). Drive to Nale Moru, hike through maize fields and forest.

Day 2: Simba Camp to Second Cave (3450m), with views of Kibo.

Day 3: Second Cave to Third Cave (3880m), short scenic hike.

Day 4: Hike to Kibo Hut (4750m), rest and prepare for summit.

Day 5: Midnight summit attempt to Uhuru Peak, descend to Horombo Hut.

Day 6: Horombo Hut to Marangu Gate. Transfer to Moshi hotel.

Price Per Person: $1,500 USD

7 Days Rongai Route Itinerary

Click to Expand

Day 1: Rongai Gate to Rongai Cave (1930m to 2835m) through forest, 3–4 hours.

Day 2: Hike via Second Cave to Kikelewa Cave (3600m), 6–8 hours.

Day 3: Short but steep trek to Mawenzi Tarn (4310m), camp at the base of Mawenzi.

Day 4: Acclimatization day with hike to Mawenzi Ridge and back.

Day 5: Mawenzi Tarn to Kibo Hut (4700m), rest for summit.

Day 6: Summit to Uhuru Peak then descend to Horombo Hut (3720m).

Day 7: Descend to Marangu Gate, receive certificate, transfer to hotel.

Price Per Person: $1,650 USD

Altitude Sickness Tips:

  • Ascend slowly – "Pole pole".
  • Climb high, sleep low.
  • Drink 4–5L of water daily.
  • If symptoms worsen, descend immediately.
