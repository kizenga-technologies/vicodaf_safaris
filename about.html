About Vicodaf Safaris

Who We Are

Vicodaf Safaris is a locally owned and operated tour company based in Arusha, Tanzania. Our mission is to deliver life-changing safari experiences by combining local expertise with international standards. From the majestic plains of the Serengeti to the peaks of Mount Kilimanjaro, we curate immersive and unforgettable journeys for every traveler.

Why Choose Vicodaf Safaris

Tailored Itineraries

We offer 100% customizable tours based on your interests, schedule, and budget.

Experienced Guides

Our guides are professional, knowledgeable and fluent in multiple languages.

Local Expertise

We are Tanzanians who know our land deeply, allowing us to give you authentic experiences.

Top Rated Service

We are committed to excellence, with countless positive reviews from satisfied travelers.

To connect people to the heart of Africa through personalized, ethical, and sustainable safari experiences.
