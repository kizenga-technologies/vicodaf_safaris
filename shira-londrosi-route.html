Shira-Londrosi Route, Kilimanjaro Trekking

Shira - Londorosi Route - Mount Kilimanjaro

The Shira - Londorosi Route offers a scenic and remote approach to Mount Kilimanjaro. It begins with a drive to the western side of the mountain, ascending through lush rainforests and crossing the Shira Plateau. This route is ideal for trekkers seeking solitude and dramatic landscapes, including breathtaking views of glaciers, moorlands, and high alpine deserts.

Best Time to Climb

The best seasons to attempt the Shira Route are from January to mid-March and from June to October when the weather is drier and more stable, offering better trail conditions and visibility.

Itinerary Highlights

  • Day 1: Drive to Shira Gate and hike to Shira 2 Camp (Rainforest)
  • Day 2: Shira Plateau to Lava Tower to Barranco Camp (Acclimatization)
  • Day 3: Barranco Wall to Karanga Camp (Alpine Desert)
  • Day 4: Trek to Barafu Camp (Summit base)
  • Day 5: Midnight summit to Uhuru Peak, descend to Mweka Camp
  • Day 6: Hike down to Mweka Gate and drive back to Moshi
