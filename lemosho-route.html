Lemosho Route

Lemosho Route - Mount Kilimanjaro Climb

The Lemosho Route is widely regarded as one of the most beautiful routes on Kilimanjaro. Offering panoramic views and a high success rate, it provides a longer itinerary for better acclimatization. This route is ideal for climbers seeking a less-crowded path and exceptional scenery.

Best Time to Climb

The best months to climb Mount Kilimanjaro via the Lemosho Route are January to March and June to October — when the weather is drier and the views are clearer.

7-Day Itinerary

View 7-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Lemosho Gate – Big Tree Camp (2780 m) – Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Day 2: Big Tree Camp – Shira Camp 2 (3900 m)

Day 3: Shira Camp 2 – Lava Tower – Barranco Camp (3960 m)

Day 4: Barranco Camp – Karanga Camp (4035 m)

Day 5: Karanga Camp – Barafu Camp (4640 m)

Day 6: Barafu – Uhuru Peak – Mweka Camp (3080 m)

Day 7: Mweka Camp – Mweka Gate – Moshi
