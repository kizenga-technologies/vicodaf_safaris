7 Days & 6 Nights Tanzania Safari

Explore the wonders of Northern Tanzania on this 7-day private safari covering four iconic destinations: Tarangire, Lake Manyara, Serengeti, and Ngorongoro Crater. Witness Africa‚Äôs Big Five, spectacular landscapes, and unique wildlife behaviors across plains, lakes, and craters. This safari is perfect for nature lovers, photographers, and adventure seekers looking for an unforgettable experience.

ü¶Å Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Tarangire National Park Game Drive

Pick up at 7:00am from your hotel in Arusha. Drive to Tarangire National Park for a full-day game drive. Enjoy sightings of elephants, baobab trees, and predators. Check out at 6:00pm and proceed to Karatu for dinner and overnight.

Day 2: Lake Manyara National Park

After breakfast, head to Lake Manyara National Park. Enjoy a picnic lunch at a scenic site and spot tree-climbing lions and flamingos. Ascend the Rift Valley wall and return to Karatu for overnight stay.

Day 3: Serengeti National Park

Depart for Serengeti after breakfast. Reach in time for an afternoon game drive. Overnight stay in the heart of the Serengeti.

Day 4: Full-Day Serengeti Game Drive

Explore the vast plains of the Serengeti on a full-day game drive. Watch predators in action, witness the migration (seasonal), and marvel at the endless savannahs. Overnight at camp.

Day 5: Another Full-Day in Serengeti

Enjoy another exciting full-day game drive in Serengeti National Park with your expert guide. Overnight at the same camp.

Day 6: Half-Day Game Drive + Transfer to Ngorongoro

Morning game drive in Serengeti, followed by lunch. Depart to Ngorongoro and check in at Rhino Lodge near the crater rim for dinner and overnight.

Day 7: Ngorongoro Crater - Arusha

Descend into the Ngorongoro Crater for an amazing wildlife experience. Picnic lunch at the hippo pool site. Ascend in the afternoon and drive back to Arusha.
